2007 Moomba Mobius LSV

A solid entry level tow boat to get you out on the water!

Introduction

The 2007 Moomba Mobius LSV is an entry level wake boat designed for ease of use, ease of maintenance, and confidence building in the wake sports arena.
With the ability to put out usable but unintimidating wake, whether it be for surfing, wakeboarding, or skiing, the 07 LSV provides the opportunity to hone your
skills across the board.


Details

The base engine for the 07 Moomba is the Indmar 325 hp mated to a v-drive. Reliable and powerful, it is also maintenance friendly.
The Rad-a-cage tower comes standard, along static with wakeboard racks. A popular upgrade these days is to replace with a surfboard option. Also standard is a beam-width swim step. The bimini top is a nice upgrade for creating some shade.
Though not standard, there are a number of options for tower mounted speakers and lights to customize your boat.
