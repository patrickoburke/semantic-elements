2007 Moomba Mobius LSV

A solid entry level tow boat to get you out on the water!

Introduction

The 2007 Moomba Mobius LSV is an entry level wake boat designed for ease of use, ease of maintenance, and confidence building in the wake sports arena.
With the ability to put out usable but unintimidating wake, whether it be for surfing, wakeboarding, or skiing, the 07 LSV provides the opportunity to hone your
skills across the board.
